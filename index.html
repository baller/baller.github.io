Hi, I am Liang Sifang, a master's student in Artificial Intelligence at the Hangzhou Institute for Advanced Study, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research focuses on image and video editing, controllable generation, multimodal understanding, and agent systems.

I am interested in vision systems that expose controllable generation interfaces, preserve semantic consistency across edits, and connect perception, reasoning, and tool use in practical multimodal workflows.

Research-oriented image editing work around portrait lighting effects, including hair light, Tyndall light, side light, sunset light, and slit light.
