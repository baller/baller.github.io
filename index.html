VIVO Research Manuscript

LightDial: Towards Controllable Portrait Lighting Editing

LightDial targets continuous, fine-grained control of portrait lighting effects while preserving identity, pose, appearance, and scene structure.

PortraitLight-50K A continuous portrait-lighting dataset spanning six photographic lighting effects and multi-strength editing sequences.
TriDial Encodes edit strength as stream-dependent Q/K rotations across text, noisy-target, and reference-image streams.
Strength-Aware Alignment Aligns the scalar control with spatial illumination and lighting semantics while constraining non-edited content.

Status Research manuscript in progress. Quantitative comparisons and ablations will be added after verification.
